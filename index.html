Instructions:

  1. Select the desired geometric characteristic from the drop-down menu.
  2. Type tolerances,datum alphabates using capital letters in the designated text area above 👆.
  3. Utilize the provided buttons to add separators, initiate, conclude & add special characters in FCF.
  4. User may also use curly braces'{}' to initiate and conclude FCF & vertical bar "|" to create box separator.
  5. clicking the change color button will cycle the text color in the textarea through red, blue, and black.
  6. When satisfied, click the "Copy PNG Img" button to copy the image to your clipboard.

Important Notes:

  1. Tip: When adding a character, use a single backspace afterward to remove the top and bottom lines, and type '_'(underscore) after the character to restore/add them.
  2. This website is not optimized for mobile screens and may not display correctly.
  3. Exercise caution: the generator allows creation of feature control frames that may violate GD&T standards.
